This Privacy Policy explains how Stef Simmons, Mortgage Broker with Barrett Financial Group, LLC ("we," "us," or "our"), collects, uses, and protects information you provide through this website.

Information We Collect

When you use the contact form on this site, we collect the information you choose to provide, such as your name, email address, phone number, and any message details. If you begin a loan application through our lender's online application system, that information is collected and handled under that system's own privacy practices.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to respond to your inquiry, discuss loan options, and follow up about your home-financing needs. We do not sell your personal information.

How Your Information Is Shared

We may share information with Barrett Financial Group, LLC and with lenders, service providers, or partners as needed to respond to your request or process a loan you initiate, and as required by law.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information submitted through this site. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Third-Party Links

This site links to third-party tools such as our online application portal and scheduling calendar. Those services have their own privacy policies, which govern the information you provide to them.
