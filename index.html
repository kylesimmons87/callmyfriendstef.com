Good to Know

Frequently asked questions

What areas do you serve for mortgages?

I serve the Tri-Cities of Washington — Kennewick, Pasco, Richland, West Richland, Benton City, Burbank, and Finley — plus communities across all of Washington. I'm also licensed and active in Oregon (including the Portland metro) and Texas, and licensed in Idaho, Arizona, and Wyoming.

Do you do mortgages in the Tri-Cities and Kennewick, WA?

Yes — I'm based in Kennewick and work with homebuyers throughout the Tri-Cities and anywhere in Washington. It's my home market.

Do you do home loans in Texas?

Yes. I'm licensed in Texas and originate a large number of Texas home loans, including for buyers relocating to or already living in Texas.

Do you still do Oregon and Portland mortgages?

Absolutely. I was originally based in Portland, Oregon and continue to do a large share of Oregon loans — across the Portland metro and statewide.

What types of home loans do you offer?

As an independent broker I offer conventional, FHA, VA, USDA, and jumbo loans, plus refinancing and cash-out options — shopping multiple lenders to find the best rate and fit for you.

How is a mortgage broker different from a bank?

Instead of a single bank's products, I work with many lenders and compare their rates and programs on your behalf — so you get options, not just one institution's answer.
